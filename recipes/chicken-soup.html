Hearty Chicken Soup

Description

This is a quick and easy chicken soup recipe for when you're feeling under the weather or just need a comforting, warm meal. It's made with simple, wholesome ingredients and comes together in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add carrots, celery, and onion. Cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add the shredded chicken and egg noodles. Cook until the noodles are tender, about 8-10 minutes.
  6. Stir in the fresh parsley.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
